About

"Crafting curated residential spaces defined by the quiet warmth of thoughtful design"

Nadia Wrotecka is a Leeds based Interior Designer and Stylist with 4 years experience creating CGI spaces for the UK's leading KBB Retailers, after acquiring experience working at Green Room Interiors producing high-end residential spaces for clients and homes across West Yorkshire.

She holds a First Class Degree in BA (Hons) Interior Design, winning Dezeen's award for the 'Most Future Focused' final year project.

Since then she has designed over 300 sets for over 30 unique brands, creating spaces that inspire trends within people's homes and injecting client products with life.
